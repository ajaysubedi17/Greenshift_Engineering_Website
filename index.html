About Us

Where Technical Rigour Meets Environmental Purpose

Green-Shift Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is a Kathmandu-based consultancy dedicated to integrating sustainable practices with advanced civil and environmental engineering. We are committed to excellence, innovation, and value-driven service delivery.

Our work bridges the gap between academic knowledge and professional practice — providing solutions that are technically precise, cost-effective, and aligned with both national standards and international best practices.

Empowering Professionals

Through innovation, digital tools, and capacity-building programs addressing the evolving challenges of infrastructure and environmental management.

Delivering Sustainable Solutions

Client-focused, practical, and aligned with both local and international standards — creating measurable value for clients, stakeholders, and communities.
